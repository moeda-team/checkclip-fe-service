## 1. Introduction

PT. Altimeda Cipta Visitama ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates AIMOS, an AI Marketing Operation System (AIMOS) — a multi-tenant platform for managing and optimizing advertising campaigns across third-party advertising networks.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, access, process, and store data when you use our platform, including our integrations with Google Ads API, Meta Marketing API, and other third-party advertising services, as well as our use of third-party artificial intelligence (AI) providers for certain data processing features.

By using AIMOS, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

**Legal Basis for Processing.** AIMOS is available to users in Indonesia, Australia, Japan, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, and other ASEAN member states. We process personal data only where a permitted basis exists under the applicable privacy law of each jurisdiction in which we operate. The key laws applicable to our users include:

(i) Indonesia: Personal Data Protection Law (UU PDP No. 27/2022);
(ii) Australia: Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs);
(iii) Japan: Act on Protection of Personal Information (APPI, as amended 2022);
(iv) Singapore: Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA, as amended 2021);
(v) Thailand: Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019); and
(vi) Malaysia: Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA Malaysia, as amended).

Where no specific legal basis regime applies in a given jurisdiction, we process personal data only for the purposes disclosed in this Privacy Policy and only to the extent necessary to provide our services. The legal bases we rely on are:

(a) **Performance of contract:** processing necessary to deliver the AIMOS platform services to you as a B2B client, including campaign management, reporting, CRM, and AI-assisted optimisation features;

(b) **Legitimate interests:** processing that is necessary for our legitimate business interests in operating, maintaining, and improving the AIMOS platform, where those interests are not overridden by your rights and interests; and

(c) **Compliance with legal obligations:** processing required to comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

Where we process personal data of consumer lead subjects (individuals whose data is submitted through your Meta Lead Ads forms), we do so as a data processor acting on your instructions as the data controller. You, as the controller, are responsible for establishing a lawful basis for that processing and for providing any required notices to those individuals.

## 3. Data We Access and Store

AIMOS connects to your existing advertising accounts (such as Google Ads and Meta Ads) via official APIs.

**Personal data collected directly from AIMOS platform users.** In addition to advertising account data accessed via third-party APIs, AIMOS also collects personal data directly from individuals who register for and use the AIMOS platform, including:

(a) **Account registration data:** name, email address, organisation name, and job title provided at sign-up;

(b) **Authentication credentials:** hashed passwords or OAuth tokens used to authenticate platform access;

(c) **Billing and subscription data:** payment information, subscription tier, and transaction history (note: full payment card details are processed by our payment provider and are not stored by AIMOS);

(d) **Usage and activity data:** login history, feature usage logs, and audit trails generated through your use of the platform;

(e) **Communications data:** content of support requests, feedback submissions, or other communications you send to us.

This data is collected to create and manage your AIMOS account, provide and improve the platform, process payments, and communicate with you about your account and our services.

AIMOS is transitioning from a purely real-time data access model to a model where certain categories of data are stored in a managed database to support features such as the CRM module, historical campaign reporting, and product data management. The table below reflects this transition:

| Data Category | Handling |
| --- | --- |
| Connected account credentials (OAuth tokens, account IDs) | Planned to be stored in our database. Not yet implemented as of this policy's last update — currently handled in memory on a session basis only, not persisted to disk or database. While in transient use, OAuth tokens are transmitted exclusively over encrypted HTTPS connections and are not logged or written to persistent storage. |
| Campaign performance and historical campaign data | Stored in our database to support historical reporting. |
| Lead / CRM data | Stored in our database as part of the CRM module (see Section 5b). |
| Product data | Stored in our database to support product management features. |

Where data is stored, it is retained for as long as your Account remains active, as described in Section 7. Where data has not yet transitioned to stored handling, it continues to be accessed directly from your connected accounts in real time, without independent long-term storage.

## 4. How We Use Your Data

The data accessed and stored through your connected advertising accounts is used exclusively for:

* Managing, monitoring, and optimizing your advertising campaigns
* Providing campaign performance reporting, historical analytics, and dashboards within the AIMOS platform
* Enabling automated or AI-assisted campaign adjustments based on performance data
* Delivering lead data submitted through your Lead Ads forms to you and maintaining that data within the AIMOS CRM module
* Cleaning, validating, and de-duplicating product and lead data through AI-assisted data enrichment (see Section 5c)
* Supporting billing and budget management features
* Improving the functionality and performance of the AIMOS platform, strictly limited to diagnosing technical issues, fixing bugs, and enhancing user-visible features — and expressly excluding any use of advertising API data (Google Ads data or Meta Ads data) to train or improve generalised artificial intelligence or machine learning models, or to profile users for purposes unrelated to their own campaigns

We do not sell, rent, or trade your Google Ads data, Meta Ads data, or other connected advertising account data to third parties.

**Data Processing Agreement.** Where AIMOS processes personal data on your behalf as a data processor (including lead and CRM data), we do so subject to a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) incorporated into our Terms of Service or available as a standalone agreement upon request. The DPA governs the scope, nature, purpose, and duration of our processing of your personal data, and includes the obligations required of us as a processor under UU PDP No. 27/2022 and applicable Australian privacy law. To request a copy of our DPA, contact [privacy@altimeda.com](mailto:privacy@altimeda.com).

We do not share, transfer, or disclose this data except where necessary to:

* Provide and maintain the platform services requested by you
* Process data through trusted service providers acting on our behalf, including cloud infrastructure providers and AI providers described in Sections 5c and 7
* Comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests
* Protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, our company, or the public

## 5. Google API Services User Data Policy

AIMOS's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

This means:

* We only use Google user data to provide or improve features visible to the user in AIMOS
* We do not use Google user data to serve advertisements
* We do not use Google user data to train or improve generalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models
* We do not allow humans to read Google user data unless required for security, legal compliance, internal operations, or with your explicit consent
* We do not use Google user data for any purpose not disclosed in this Privacy Policy

For more information: https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy

**Google data and AI processing.** Google-sourced data (including data retrieved via the Google Ads API) is excluded from processing by third-party AI providers described in Section 5c. AIMOS does not transmit Google user data to OpenAI, Anthropic, or any other external AI provider. This exclusion is maintained as a standing operational control to ensure compliance with Google's API Services User Data Policy Limited Use requirements.

## 5b. Lead Ads Data Handling

If you use Meta Lead Ads in connection with AIMOS, we retrieve lead submission data (such as name, email, and phone number) via the leads_retrieval permission to deliver this data to you within the AIMOS platform and to populate the AIMOS CRM module on your behalf.

* Lead data is retrieved and stored on your behalf within your tenant workspace, and is not used by AIMOS for any independent marketing purpose, or shared with any other client of AIMOS
* Lead data may be processed by third-party AI providers solely for data cleanup, validation, and de-duplication purposes, as described in Section 5c
* Lead data is not sold, rented, or shared with any third party other than the service providers necessary to operate AIMOS, including the AI providers described in Section 5c
* Lead data is retained for as long as your Account remains active, and following account closure, is retained in archive for thirty (30) days and then permanently deleted, as described in Section 7
* You are responsible for ensuring your own use of lead data submitted through your Lead Ads forms complies with applicable data protection laws, including obtaining any consent required from data subjects for the processing described in this Privacy Policy.

**Notice to consumer lead data subjects.** AIMOS processes the personal data of individuals who submit their information through your Lead Ads forms (lead subjects) as a data processor acting on your instructions. Lead subjects are not direct users of AIMOS and are not parties to this Privacy Policy. As the data controller, you are required to:

(a) provide lead subjects with a clear and accessible privacy notice at the point of data collection, disclosing how their data will be processed and by whom (including that it will be stored and processed within AIMOS and may be transmitted to AI service providers for data validation purposes);

(b) obtain any consent required by applicable law before collecting and processing lead subject data; and

(c) ensure that lead subject data is not retained within AIMOS for longer than is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

AIMOS will process lead subject data only in accordance with your instructions and will not use it for any purpose of its own beyond those described in this Privacy Policy.

## 6. Google OAuth Permissions and Scope of Access

When you connect your Google Ads account to AIMOS, we request access through OAuth 2.0 authorization.

The specific permission currently requested is:

* `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/adwords`

This scope allows AIMOS to read and manage Google Ads campaigns, ad groups, ads, keywords, and related settings.

As AIMOS introduces new features, we may request additional scopes. Any new scope will be presented to you for consent during the OAuth authorization flow and reflected in an updated version of this Privacy Policy.

You may review or revoke permissions at any time through: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

## 6a. Meta OAuth Permissions and Scope of Access

When you connect your Meta Ads account to AIMOS, we request access through Meta's OAuth authorization flow.

The specific permissions currently requested are:

* `ads_management` — create and manage campaigns, ad sets, and ads on your behalf
* `ads_read` — retrieve campaign performance metrics
* `business_management` — access Business Manager assets (ad accounts, Pages) necessary for campaign operations
* `pages_show_list` — view the list of Pages you manage, so you can select which Page to run campaigns for
* `pages_manage_ads` — create and manage ads associated with your Page
* `pages_read_engagement` — read engagement data on your Page for reporting purposes
* `leads_retrieval` — retrieve lead data submitted through your Lead Ads forms
* `public_profile` — access your basic profile information for account authentication purposes

As AIMOS introduces new features, including expanded CRM capabilities, we may request additional permissions. Any new permission will be presented to you for consent during the OAuth authorization flow and reflected in an updated version of this Privacy Policy.

You may review or revoke these permissions at any time through: https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools

## 7. Data Storage, Retention, and International Transfer

AIMOS is transitioning from a model where advertising data was accessed only in real time, to a model where certain categories of data — including connected account information, campaign history, lead/CRM records, and product data — are stored in a managed database to support reporting, CRM, and related features, as described in Section 3.

**Retention while your Account is active.** Stored data is retained for as long as your Account remains active and your subscription is in good standing. There is no fixed maximum retention period while your Account is active.

**Retention after account closure.** When your Account is closed or your subscription ends, your stored data is retained in archive for thirty (30) days, after which it is permanently deleted from our systems, except where we are required by law to retain certain records for a longer period.

**International data storage.** Our database infrastructure is hosted with international cloud service providers (which may include providers such as AWS, Google Cloud Platform, or Microsoft Azure, depending on the specific service). This means your data, including personal data, may be processed and stored outside of Indonesia. Where this is the case, we take steps to ensure such data continues to receive an appropriate level of protection, consistent with the cross-border data transfer requirements of Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law (UU PDP No. 27/2022) Art. 56. These steps include requiring that international cloud and AI service providers we engage are bound by data processing agreements that impose data protection standards at least equivalent to those required under UU PDP and applicable Australian privacy law. We will not transfer personal data to a country that does not provide an adequate level of protection without first implementing one of the transfer mechanisms recognised under applicable law (such as standard contractual clauses or binding corporate rules). Where required, we will disclose the countries to which data is transferred upon request to [privacy@altimeda.com](mailto:privacy@altimeda.com).

**Temporary and session-based data.** Where data has not yet transitioned to the stored model described above, it continues to be processed in real time and on a session basis, and is not retained beyond what is necessary for platform functionality.

If you have questions regarding data retention or international data transfer, please contact us at: [privacy@altimeda.com](mailto:privacy@altimeda.com)

## 9. Data Security

We implement the following technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

* **Encryption in transit:** all data transmitted between your browser, the AIMOS platform, and third-party APIs is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
* OAuth 2.0 authentication
* **Encryption at rest:** sensitive stored data (including OAuth tokens when stored, lead data, and personal account information) is encrypted at rest using AES-256 or equivalent industry-standard cipher
* Access controls for authorised personnel only
* **Security reviews:** we conduct security assessments of our platform and infrastructure at least annually, or following any material change to our data handling practices, including vulnerability scanning of production systems

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet, or storage, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

**Data Breach Notification.** In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in harm to affected individuals, we will:

(a) notify the relevant supervisory authority as soon as practicable, and in any event within the shortest timeframe required by the applicable law of the affected jurisdiction, including:

* Indonesia: within 3 x 24 hours (72 hours) under UU PDP No. 27/2022 Art. 46;
* Australia: as required under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme (Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) Part IIIC);
* Japan: without delay (promptly) to the Personal Information Protection Commission (PPC) under APPI;
* Singapore: within 3 calendar days of assessing a breach as notifiable to the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) under the PDPA (Singapore);
* Thailand: within 72 hours to the Office of the Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC Thailand) under the PDPA Thailand;
* Malaysia: within 72 hours to the Commissioner under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Malaysia), where required;

(b) notify affected individuals without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and interests, in accordance with the requirements of the applicable jurisdiction;

(c) document all breaches internally, including those that do not meet the notification threshold in any jurisdiction, for accountability and regulatory purposes.

If you believe your data has been involved in a security incident, please contact us immediately at: [privacy@altimeda.com](mailto:privacy@altimeda.com)
